ItemIDs are used to reprsent Items. In Yo-kai Watch 2 there are 5 item types:
* `Creature` - Critters
* `Important` - Key Items
* `Soul` - Souls
* `Equipment` - Non-Soul Equippables
* `Consume` - Everything Else.
Not to be confused with `InventorySort` or `ItemType`.
