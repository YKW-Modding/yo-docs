# Playing YW1 Mobile Mods (Android)

### Prerequisites
[Smartphone Mod Tools](https://github.com/StringsVR/YKW1-Smartphone-Mod-Tools/releases/download/v1.2/release.zip)

### Guide
Click the link above, to download SMT. Next, extract the contents of the zip file into a (preferrably empty; to avoid any of your files from potentially being overwritten) folder and double click "YKW1S-Mod-Tools.exe" from inside that folder. 

Now extract your .APKS file from your android device. You can either extract it via your own means (Lucky Patcher, APK Extractor, etc) and move it to your PC. Or you can grab via ADB, to do this you have to have USB Debugging turned on and have a USB connected from your phone to your device. All you have to do is click File -> Install ADB & File -> Import .APKS from ADB. After this, click Decompile. 

Once done, click "Open Modding Panel", then import your .YKM (the standard mod file for smartphone), after importing just click inject on your specific mod and boom its injected. Then just Build, Merge, Sign and export!
